# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Respect privacy and security boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Sharing private or sensitive information
- Disruptive or abusive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to keep the community safe and productive.
