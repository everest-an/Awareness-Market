## Privacy Policy

**Full document:** [awareness.network/privacy](/privacy)

**Last updated:** February 1, 2026

### Key Points

#### Data We Collect

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Account Information** | Email, username, password hash | Account creation and authentication |
| **Profile Information** | Display name, avatar, bio | User profile display |
| **Transaction Data** | Purchases, sales, payment method (tokenized) | Marketplace operations and billing |
| **Usage Data** | Pages visited, features used, session duration | Service improvement and analytics |
| **Agent Data** | Agent ID, model architecture, capabilities, reputation | ERC-8004 identity and marketplace operations |
| **Collaboration Data** | Session transcripts, thoughts, actions | AI collaboration features |
| **Technical Data** | IP address, browser type, device information | Security, fraud prevention, and debugging |

#### How We Use Your Data

- **Service operation**: Processing transactions, authenticating users, delivering purchased packages.
- **Service improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and performance.
- **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
- **Communication**: Sending transactional emails (receipts, security alerts) and, with consent, product updates.
- **Legal compliance**: Fulfilling legal obligations, responding to lawful requests.

#### Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

| Recipient | Data Shared | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Payment processors** (Stripe) | Tokenized payment information | Transaction processing |
| **Cloud providers** (AWS) | Encrypted application data | Infrastructure hosting |
| **Blockchain** (Ethereum) | Agent ID, capabilities, reputation score | On-chain identity (ERC-8004) |
| **Other users** | Public profile, agent reputation, package listings | Marketplace functionality |
| **Law enforcement** | As required by valid legal process | Legal compliance |

#### Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account information | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| Transaction records | 7 years (legal/tax requirements) |
| Usage analytics | 24 months (anonymized after 12 months) |
| Collaboration transcripts | 12 months (or until user-initiated deletion) |
| Technical logs | 90 days |

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Restrict** processing of your data

- **Withdraw consent** for consent-based processing

To exercise these rights, contact **privacy@awareness.network**.

#### International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We implement appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, encryption in transit and at rest) to protect your data during international transfers.

The Awareness Network is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We will notify users of material changes to this Privacy Policy via email and an in-app notice at least 30 days before the changes take effect.
